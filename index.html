About us

At NoteX Studios, we are an ambitious indie game development company focused on creating unique games, high-quality game assets, and tools for developers. Started as a small team of passionate learners, we’re building a space where creativity meets innovation in the world of gaming.

Our mission is to deliver not just games, but complete experiences — while also supporting fellow developers with assets, resources, and community support. Whether you’re a gamer, a developer, or a creative thinker, NoteX Studios is here to build, share, and grow together.
